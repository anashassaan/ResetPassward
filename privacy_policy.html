Al Quran App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Al Quran ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up with email/password or Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as Surahs read, bookmarks saved, and language preferences.
  • Device Information: We may collect your device type and operating system version for app compatibility purposes.
  • No Financial Data: We do not collect or store any payment or financial information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Al Quran app
  • Save your reading progress and bookmarks across devices
  • Personalise your experience (display name, avatar, theme)
  • Improve, personalise, and expand our app features
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Monitor app usage to fix bugs and improve performance

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Appwrite Cloud infrastructure hosted in Singapore. We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your session data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Appwrite Cloud — backend authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In — optional OAuth authentication
  • Open-Source Quranic Data — no network calls, stored locally

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Quran Data

All Quranic text, translations, and page image data used in this app are from authentic, open-source sources. We do not monetise or sell Quranic content. All content is provided purely for the purpose of facilitating recitation, understanding, and memorisation of the Holy Quran.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, you can delete your account from the Profile screen, or contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at: github.com/anashassaan

This app is developed with the sole intention of making the words of Allah accessible to every Muslim around the world. May Allah accept it from us.
